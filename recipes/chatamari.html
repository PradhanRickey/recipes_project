Chatamari

Description:

This is a recipe passed down by generations in the Nepali culture.
It is a deliciously crafted dish which contains simple indredients
with added spices for flavor. This dish is maily comes from the
Newari culutre of nepal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground turkey
  • 1 cup Rice Flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 Onions
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 tbsp Grated Ginger
  • 1 tbsp Grated Garlic
  • Cilantro for garnish
  • Serrano Peppers (per preference of spicy level)
  • 1tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 1tsp Cumin
  • 1tsp Tumeric
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat up a medium skillet pan Over medium heat
  2. Pour 3 tablespoons of oil
  3. Throw in the turkey and add the tumeric for 10 mins breaking it down
  4. Add onions and cool until soft
  5. Add minced ginger and garlic and cook until fragrant (30seconds)
  6. Add spices salt and pepper while stirring the meat
  7. add the tomatoes and cook until soft
  8. add a cup of water and cook until the water is evaporated stirring occationally
  9. Add a cup of rice flower in a large bowl
  10. Slowly add water until it reaches pancake batter consistency
  11. In a new pan add 1tbsp of preferred oil
  12. Pour a thin layer of batter onto the pan
  13. Sprinkle the prepared meat onto the batter covering most of the surface
  14. Crack an egg in the middle of the chatamari
  15. Cover the pan and cook under low heat until the egg is cooked to your liking
