Turkey Burger

Description:

This is one of my favorite recipes at the moment. It does not
take long to prepare and packs alot of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground turkey
  • Lettuce
  • 1/2 Onions
  • 1/2 Tomato
  • 1tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • preferred hotsauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine the ingredients in a bowl
  2. Form patties with the turkey
  3. Heat up a medium skillet pan over medium heat
  4. Pour 3 tablespoons of oil
  5. Place turkey patties in the pan and press down
  6. Cook for 15 mins on both sides
  7. Add preferred topping (cheese/avocado/more tomatoes/onions
  8. Wrap in lettuce and serve!
