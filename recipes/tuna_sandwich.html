Chatamari

Description:

This is a one of those recipes that you do not have to heat up or cook.
It is perfect for a quick lunch and packs a ton of protien.

Ingredients

  • 1 can tuna
  • 1/2 can corn
  • 1 Preferred bagel
  • 1oz Cream Cheese
  • 1tsp Minced Garlic
  • 1tsp Paprika
  • 2 tbsp spicy Mayo
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Open the can of tuna using a can opener
  2. Combine with spicy mayo and corn in a small bowl
  3. Combine creamcheese, garlic, and paprica in separate bowl
  4. Spread the creamcheese onto each slice of bagel
  5. Toast the bagel if preferred
  6. Add the tuna on top of the bagel
